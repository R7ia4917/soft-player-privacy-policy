Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

In short: Soft Player does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Your media library and preferences stay on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how R7ia4917 ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Soft Player Android application (package name com.r7ia4917.softplayer).

Information Soft Player accesses

Soft Player may request permission to read videos stored on your device. On supported Android versions, you may grant access to all videos or only selected videos. This access is used to display your video library, organize videos into folders, generate thumbnails, and play the media you choose.

When another app sends a video to Soft Player, the app receives a temporary or persistent Android content URI permission so it can play that item. Soft Player may read basic media metadata such as the file name, folder, duration, size, resolution, and modification time.

Local storage

Soft Player stores a local media index and app preferences, including appearance settings, on your device. This information is not uploaded to us. You can remove it by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

Internet access

Internet access is used only when you open a network media URL or when Google Play checks for and delivers an app update. When you open a remote stream, your device connects directly to the server hosting that media. That server may receive standard connection information such as your IP address and may apply its own privacy policy.

Data collection and sharing

Soft Player has no user accounts, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or developer-operated backend. We do not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share your media library, viewing activity, or personal information.

Third-party services

Soft Player uses Google Play's in-app update service. Google may process device and app information according to the Google Privacy Policy. Remote media providers you choose to access are independent third parties.

Data security

Media information and preferences are kept in Android's app-private storage. No method of storage is completely risk-free, but Soft Player minimizes risk by keeping this information local and requesting only the access needed for playback and library features.

Children's privacy

Soft Player does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not collect personal data from any user, it does not create profiles of children.

Your choices

  • Grant access to all videos or selected videos, where Android supports this choice.
  • Revoke media access from Android system settings.
  • Clear locally stored app data from Android system settings.
  • Avoid opening remote URLs if you do not want to contact third-party servers.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app's features or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are published.
